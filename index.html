Why Use Our Fitness Calculators?

Whether you're trying to lose weight, build muscle, or simply live healthier, understanding your body's needs is the first step. Our free calculators give you the numbers you need to make informed decisions.

  • 100% Free — No sign-up, no email, no hidden fees.
  • Privacy First — All calculations run in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

  • Mobile Friendly — Use them on any device, anywhere.

Understanding Your Body's Needs

Everyone's body is different. Factors like age, weight, height, gender, and activity level all affect how many calories you burn and what nutrients you need. Our calculators take these factors into account to give you personalized estimates.

Start with the TDEE Calculator to understand your daily calorie needs, then use the Macro Calculator to break those calories into protein, carbs, and fat. From there, fine-tune with our other tools.
