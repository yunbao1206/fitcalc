About WiseClick

Our Mission

WiseClick exists to help people understand their health metrics with free, science-based tools. We believe everyone deserves access to clear, accurate health information — no sign-ups, no subscriptions, no hidden costs. Whether you're starting a fitness journey, managing your weight, or simply curious about your body's needs, our calculators give you the numbers you need instantly.

What We Offer

WiseClick provides 10 free health and fitness calculators, each built on established scientific formulas:

  • TDEE Calculator — Estimate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure to understand how many calories you burn each day
  • BMI Calculator — Calculate your Body Mass Index and see where you fall on the standard scale
  • Calorie Deficit Calculator — Plan a safe and effective calorie deficit for weight loss
  • Macro Calculator — Determine your optimal balance of proteins, carbs, and fats
  • Body Fat Calculator — Estimate your body fat percentage using simple measurements
  • Protein Calculator — Find out how much protein you need based on your goals and activity level
  • Water Intake Calculator — Calculate your recommended daily water intake
  • BMR Calculator — Find your Basal Metabolic Rate to understand your calorie needs at rest
  • Ideal Weight Calculator — See estimated ideal weight ranges based on multiple scientific formulas
  • Calories Burned Calculator — Estimate calories burned during various exercises and activities

Our Principles

  • Free Forever — All calculators are completely free to use. No premium tiers, no paywalls, no email required.
  • Privacy First — Every calculation runs directly in your browser. Your health data never leaves your device. We collect zero personal health information.
  • Science-Based Formulas — Our calculators use well-established formulas such as Mifflin-St Jeor, Harris-Benedict, and Jackson-Pollock, published in peer-reviewed research.
  • No Medical Claims — WiseClick provides estimates for educational purposes. We do not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice.

Important Disclaimer

WiseClick is NOT a medical device. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The results provided by our calculators are estimates and should not be used to make medical decisions. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any diet, exercise, or weight loss program.
